Target audience

The site is intended for people who are part of the chamber and would like to get some information about it. It will also be helpful for other local businesses that may want to join the chamber.

Personas

Persona 1: Martha, a 45 year old woman who owns a store in town. She has studied in an institute but doesn't have a bachelor's degree. She is married and has 3 children. Her objective is to be a part of the chamber to access its benefits and also make herself known in the community.

Persona 2: Carlos is a 36 year old medic who is already part of the chamber. He has a bachelor's degree in general medicine, and would like to study the policies of the chamber to establish a new consultory and hire some other doctors. He is married with no kids.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Martha wants to connect with other local businesses which are members of the Chamber who would like to sell spots for publicity in her walls

Scenario 2: Martha wants to know the calendar of activities to prepare discounts for those days and bring specific products.

Scenario 3: Martha wants to find out which trainings are available for her to take and become more popular in town.

Scenario 4: Carlos wants to make himself known among the local medicine students that may be interested in doing their internships with him.

Scenario 5: Carlos wants to make himself known among the local people to increase his clients.
